Top Bar

The top bar contains the title of the manual and Search, Table of Contents (TOC) and Settings charm icons. The three charm icons on the right of the top bar are Search, TOC and Settings.

The top bar has a fixed height of 50px and a horizontal linear gradient background with $dark-blue to $medium-blue color-stops. It is the only section of the CVA that will never have any custom styling.

On layouts smaller than 940px, or $topbar-breakpoint, the 'Settings' icon in the top bar is hidden and the settings navigation is combined with the TOC.
